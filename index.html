What is Antaragni Leadership Initiative?

The youth are the building blocks of a country. They have the courage and energy to question the established systems and to try out new ideas. This is exactly what we plan to accomplish through the Antaragni Leadership Initiative.

The three month long campaign serves as the perfect platform for young leaders to voice their opinions on issues which directly affect our lives. It seeks to encourage them to participate in discourses with their peers on matters concerning our nation and to come up with innovative and practical solutions to seemingly inextricable problems. Our motive is to bring out young minds who can lead a change in the society by discovering the passionate leaders within themselves.
